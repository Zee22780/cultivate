How to Use This Flashcard App


Cultivate helps you learn almost any topic by creating flashcards. Just follow these instructions:


  • Click "Make A Flashcard" to make all of your flashcards. Be sure to include a Collection name for each card.

  • Click All Flashcards to view all of your flashcards in one place.

  • Click an individual flashcard to start studying your flashcards.

  • Once inside the flashcard, hover over the card to toggle between the front and back of the card.

  • If you created the card, you will have the ability to update and delete cards.

  • Click on the respective buttons to go the next or previous card.
